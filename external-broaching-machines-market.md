<p><p>External broaching machines can be classified into two main types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical external broaching machines are designed to perform broaching operations where the workpiece is held vertically and the broach tool moves vertically downwards. Horizontal external broaching machines, on the other hand, are oriented horizontally, with the workpiece held horizontally and the broach tool moving horizontally. Each type of machine is suited for different types of broaching operations and workpiece configurations.</p></p>
