<p><p>Power Distribution Units (PDUs) for data centers come in four main types: Basic PDUs, Metering PDUs, Monitoring PDUs, and Switch PDUs. Basic PDUs provide a simple distribution of power without any additional features. Metering PDUs offer the ability to measure power consumption at the outlet level. Monitoring PDUs include remote monitoring capabilities for real-time insight into power usage. Switch PDUs add the functionality of remote power cycling and outlet control. Each type offers different levels of power management and control for data center environments.</p></p>
