<p><strong>The Chip On Film Underfill (COF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill (NUF)</li><li>Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill</li><li>Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill (MUF) Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip On Film Underfill (COF) market types include Capillary Underfill (CUF), No Flow Underfill (NUF), Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill, Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill, and Molded Underfill (MUF). </p><p>CUF involves filling the gap between the chip and the substrate through capillary action. NUF does not flow after application and relies on pressure to spread. NCP uses a non-conductive adhesive to fill the gap. NCF utilizes a non-conductive film to encapsulate the chip. MUF involves molding a material around the chip for protection.</p></p>
