<p><strong>The Steel Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel coupling market has two main types: rigid coupling and flexible coupling. Rigid couplings provide a strong and permanent connection between two shafts, ensuring precise alignment and high torque transmission. On the other hand, flexible couplings allow for slight misalignment between shafts, reducing vibration and shock loads. They are ideal for applications where shafts may not be perfectly aligned or where there is a need for torsional flexibility. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial applications.</p></p>
