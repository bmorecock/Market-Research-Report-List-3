<p><p>New Energy Vehicle Airbag Inflator Market offers three types of inflators: Pyrotechnic Inflator, Stored Gas Inflator, and Hybrid Inflator. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to generate gas for airbag deployment. Stored Gas inflators store compressed gas which is released upon activation. Hybrid inflators combine elements of pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators for efficient airbag deployment. Each type of inflator offers distinct advantages and is utilized based on the specific needs and requirements of the new energy vehicle industry.</p></p>
