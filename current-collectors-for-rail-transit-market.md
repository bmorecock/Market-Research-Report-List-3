<p><p>Current collectors for rail transit can be categorized into three main types based on their running position on the track. Top-running collectors are mounted on the top of the train and collect power from the overhead wire system. Bottom-running collectors are mounted underneath the train and collect power from the third rail. Side-running collectors are mounted on the side of the train and collect power from a side-mounted rail or contact wire. Each type has its own advantages and applications in different rail transit systems.</p></p>
