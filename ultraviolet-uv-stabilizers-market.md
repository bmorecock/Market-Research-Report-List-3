<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers are additives used in plastics, coatings, and other materials to protect them from degradation caused by UV radiation. There are different types of UV stabilizers, including Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV Absorbers, Quenchers, and others. HALS work by scavenging free radicals produced by UV radiation, UV absorbers absorb and dissipate UV rays, Quenchers stop the photochemical processes induced by UV light, and other stabilizers provide unique mechanisms of protection. Each type of UV stabilizer offers specific benefits in different applications.</p></p>
