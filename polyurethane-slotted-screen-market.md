<p><p>Polyurethane slotted screens are commonly used in various industries for solid-liquid separation. They are available in different market types such as mosaic, bolted connection, bead connection, and screen hook connection. Mosaic screens consist of small individual panels that are interlocked, while bolted connection screens are secured with bolts. Bead connection screens have a bead on the edge for easy installation, and screen hook connection screens have hooks for attachment. Each market type offers unique advantages and is suitable for specific applications in the industry.</p></p>
