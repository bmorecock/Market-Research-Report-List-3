<p><p>Newborn and prenatal genetic testing involves various types of tests to detect genetic disorders and abnormalities in fetuses and newborns. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR), Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH), array Comparative Genomic Hybridization (aCGH), Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT), and Maternal Serum Screening (MSS) are commonly used methods in this market. PCR amplifies and detects DNA sequences, FISH identifies chromosomal abnormalities, aCGH detects copy number variations, NIPT analyzes fetal DNA in maternal blood, and MSS screens for markers of genetic disorders in the mother's blood.</p></p>
