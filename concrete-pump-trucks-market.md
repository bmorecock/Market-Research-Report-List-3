<p><strong>The Concrete Pump Trucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Mode</li><li>Squeeze Mode</li><li>Hydraulic Diaphragm Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete pump trucks come in different types depending on their pumping mechanism. The Piston Mode utilizes reciprocating pistons to push the concrete through the pipeline. The Squeeze Mode uses rollers to compress the hose, forcing the concrete through. The Hydraulic Diaphragm Mode relies on a diaphragm to push the concrete. Each mode has its advantages and is suited to different types of construction projects, with the choice often depending on factors such as the distance, height, and volume of concrete needed to be pumped.</p></p>
