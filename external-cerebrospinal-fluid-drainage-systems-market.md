<p><p>External Cerebrospinal Fluid Drainage Systems are medical devices used to drain excess cerebrospinal fluid from the brain or spinal cord. External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems are typically used for draining fluid from the ventricles of the brain, while Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems are used for draining fluid from the lumbar region of the spinal cord. Both systems help in relieving pressure on the brain or spinal cord caused by conditions such as hydrocephalus or trauma.</p></p>
