<p><ul><li>Polymeric</li><li>Porcelain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surge arrester market includes two main types: polymeric and porcelain. Polymeric surge arresters are made from silicone rubber and offer advantages such as lightweight, easy handling, and resistance to pollution. They are commonly used in high contamination areas. On the other hand, porcelain surge arresters are made from ceramic materials and are known for their high mechanical strength and excellent electrical performance. They are commonly used in high voltage applications where reliability is crucial. Both types offer surge protection for electrical systems by diverting excess current to ground.</p></p>
