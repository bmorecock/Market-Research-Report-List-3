<p><p>Paint Circulation System (PCS) is used in paint shops to circulate paint from storage tanks to paint booths efficiently. There are various types of PCS markets, including Two-pipe System, which involves separate lines for paint supply and return; Dead End System, where paint is pushed through one line and returns through another; Third Line System, which includes an additional line for returning unused paint; and other systems that may involve different configurations to suit specific requirements of paint circulation in industrial settings.</p></p>
