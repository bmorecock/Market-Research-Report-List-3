<p><p>Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) lithography systems are widely used in the semiconductor industry for creating high-resolution patterns on silicon wafers. The market is divided into three main types: ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, and KrF. ArF Immersion systems use a liquid medium to enhance resolution, while ArF Dry systems do not require a liquid medium, making them more cost-effective. KrF systems use a different wavelength of light to achieve similar results. Each type of DUV lithography system has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
