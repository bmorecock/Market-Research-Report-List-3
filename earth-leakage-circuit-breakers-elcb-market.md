<p><p>Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (ELCB) are safety devices that automatically disconnect electrical circuits from the power supply in case of electrical leakage. There are two main types of ELCBs based on their operation: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (CELCB). VELCBs operate by detecting voltage imbalances, while CELCBs detect current leakages. Both types are crucial in preventing electrical accidents and protecting against electric shocks, making them essential components in electrical safety systems.</p></p>
