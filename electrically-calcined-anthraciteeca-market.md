Gas Calcined Anthracite is produced by heating anthracite in gas-fired furnaces, while Electrically Calcined Anthracite is produced by passing an electric current through anthracite to achieve the desired properties. Both types are used in various industries such as steel production and electrode manufacturing.</p></p>
