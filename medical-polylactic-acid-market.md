<p><p>Medical Polylactic Acid (PLA) Market Types include L-Polylactic (PLLA), D-Polylactic (PDLA), and DL-Polylactic (PDLLA). PLLA is a type of PLA derived from L-lactic acid and exhibits high strength and stiffness. PDLA, derived from D-lactic acid, offers improved flexibility and elongation properties. PDLLA, a racemic mixture of L- and D-lactic acids, provides a balance between strength and flexibility. These different types of PLA cater to various medical applications, offering a wide range of material properties for different needs.</p></p>
