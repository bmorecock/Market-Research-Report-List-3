<p><p>A Battery Pack Management System (BPMS) can be categorized into wired and wireless markets based on how the system communicates with the batteries. In wired BPMS, communication is done through physical wires connecting the BPMS to the batteries, ensuring a secure and reliable connection. On the other hand, wireless BPMS uses wireless technology such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to communicate with the batteries, allowing for more flexibility and ease of installation.
