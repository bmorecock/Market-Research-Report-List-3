<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearings</li><li>Angular Contact Ball Bearings</li><li>Self-Aligning Ball Bearings</li><li>Thrust Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball bearing market consists of various types to cater to different applications. Deep Groove Ball Bearings are commonly used for high-speed and radial loads. Angular Contact Ball Bearings are designed to handle both radial and thrust loads simultaneously. Self-Aligning Ball Bearings can compensate for misalignment in shafts. Thrust Ball Bearings are ideal for applications requiring high axial load capacity. Each type serves a specific purpose in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machinery.</p></p>
