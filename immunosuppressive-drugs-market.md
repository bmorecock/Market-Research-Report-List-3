Antiproliferative Agents inhibit the growth of immune cells. mTOR Inhibitors target a protein to suppress the immune response. Antibodies directly target specific immune cells. Steroids reduce inflammation and suppress the immune system. Each type of immunosuppressive drug plays a crucial role in preventing rejection in organ transplantation and treating autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
