<p><p>The Truck Volume Measurement System market consists of two main types: Static Measurement System and Dynamic Measurement System. Static Measurement Systems are fixed devices installed along roadsides or in designated areas to measure truck volumes accurately. Dynamic Measurement Systems use advanced technologies such as sensors and cameras to measure truck volumes in real-time as they pass by. Both systems are vital for transportation authorities and logistics companies to efficiently manage and optimize traffic flow and monitor road usage.</p></p>
