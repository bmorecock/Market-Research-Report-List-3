<p><strong>The Embedded Middleware Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real-time Embedded Systems</li><li>Standalone Embedded Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Real-time Embedded Systems refer to systems that have strict timing requirements and need to deliver results within a specified timeframe. They are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation where timing is critical. Standalone Embedded Systems, on the other hand, do not have strict timing requirements and can operate independently without the need for real-time responsiveness. They are often used in consumer electronics, home appliances, and other devices where real-time processing is not necessary.</p></p>
