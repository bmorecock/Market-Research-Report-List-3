<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform System</li><li>Jack Weigh System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platform System and Jack Weigh System are two types of civil aircraft weighing machinery. Platform System involves using large platforms with built-in load cells to weigh an aircraft. This system is efficient for weighing larger aircraft. On the other hand, Jack Weigh System utilizes hydraulic jacks placed under the aircraft's wheels to measure the weight. This system is commonly used for smaller aircraft and allows for more precise weight measurements. Both systems play a critical role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations.</p></p>
