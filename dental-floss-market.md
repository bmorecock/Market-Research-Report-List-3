<p><p>Dental floss market includes two main types: waxed flosses and unwaxed floss. Waxed flosses are coated with a layer of wax, making it easier to glide between teeth and less likely to break. It is ideal for people with tightly spaced teeth. Unwaxed floss, on the other hand, is made of nylon or silk and does not have any coating. It is preferred by those who do not like the feeling of wax on their teeth or have more widely spaced teeth.</p></p>
