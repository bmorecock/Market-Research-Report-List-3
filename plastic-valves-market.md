Butterfly valves are characterized by a rotating disc to regulate flow, widely used in industries like water treatment and HVAC systems. Other types of plastic valves include ball valves, check valves, and diaphragm valves, each serving specific purposes in industries such as chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage.</p></p>
