<p><p>Measuring stirrers market types include Axial Flow Agitators and Radial Flow Agitators. Axial Flow Agitators are designed to produce flow parallel to the shaft and are suitable for applications requiring high efficiency mixing. Radial Flow Agitators, on the other hand, produce flow perpendicular to the shaft, providing better radial mixing. Both types of stirrers are used in various industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing for mixing, blending, dispersion, and suspension of materials.</p></p>
