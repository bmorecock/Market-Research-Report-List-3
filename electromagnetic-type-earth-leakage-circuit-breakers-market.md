<p><p>Electromagnetic-type Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers are available in two main types: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (CELCB). VELCBs detect voltage imbalances between the live and neutral conductors, while CELCBs detect current imbalances. VELCBs are more commonly used in residential and commercial settings to protect against electric shock, while CELCBs are often used in industrial applications where higher currents are present. Both types of circuit breakers play a crucial role in preventing electrical accidents and ensuring safety.</p></p>
