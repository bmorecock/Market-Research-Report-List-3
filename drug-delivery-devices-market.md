<p><p>Drug delivery devices market includes three main types: injection type, mucosal type, and implanted type. Injection type devices deliver medication through needles or syringes directly into the body. Mucosal type devices administer drugs through mucous membranes such as the lungs, nasal passages, or mouth. Implanted type devices are inserted into the body to deliver medication over an extended period of time. Each type offers unique benefits and is utilized for different medical conditions based on the most effective delivery method.</p></p>
