<p><strong>The Non-Woven Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBC-based Adhesives</li><li>APAO-based Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-woven adhesive market comprises two major types - SBC-based adhesives and APAO-based adhesives. SBC-based adhesives are known for their superior tackiness and elasticity, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and high initial adhesion. On the other hand, APAO-based adhesives offer excellent cohesive strength and are commonly used in applications requiring heat resistance and long-term durability. Both types of adhesives are widely used in non-woven applications such as hygiene products, medical supplies, and automotive interiors.</p></p>
