Sequencing by synthesis is a widely used method that involves detecting the incorporation of nucleotides during DNA synthesis. Nanopore sequencing uses nanopores to analyze nucleic acids, offering advantages such as long read lengths. Ion semiconductor sequencing measures the release of hydrogen ions during DNA synthesis. These different market types offer unique benefits and are utilized for various applications in miRNA sequencing and analysis.</p></p>
