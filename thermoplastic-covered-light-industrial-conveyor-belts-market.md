<p><p>Thermoplastic covered light industrial conveyor belts come in various types such as PU, PVC, TPEE, TPU, PE, and others. PU (Polyurethane) belts are known for their high wear resistance, while PVC (Polyvinyl chloride) belts offer good chemical resistance. TPEE (Thermoplastic Polyester Elastomer) belts provide flexibility and impact resistance, while TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) belts are durable and offer good abrasion resistance. PE (Polyethylene) belts are lightweight and have low friction properties. Each type caters to specific industrial applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
