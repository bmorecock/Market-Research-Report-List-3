<p><ul><li>PPG Backbone</li><li>PEG Backbone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyether diamines are a type of compound commonly used in various industrial applications. They can be classified into two main types based on their backbone structure: PPG backbone and PEG backbone. PPG backbone polyether diamines are derived from propylene oxide and are known for their high stability and flexibility. On the other hand, PEG backbone polyether diamines are derived from ethylene oxide and are valued for their water solubility and biocompatibility. Both types find uses in adhesives, coatings, elastomers, and sealants.</p></p>
