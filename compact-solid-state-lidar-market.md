<p><strong>The Compact Solid state LIDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compact solid-state LIDAR technology is evolving with three main market types: MEMS based scanning, phased array, and non-scanning flash. MEMS based scanning LIDAR systems use tiny mirrors on a micro-electro-mechanical system for precise beam scanning. Phased array LIDAR employs electronically controlled phase shifts to steer the laser beams. Non-scanning flash LIDAR emits multiple laser pulses simultaneously to capture a wide field of view without mechanical scanning. These advancements in compact LIDAR technology are revolutionizing the autonomous driving, robotics, and industrial applications industries.</p></p>
