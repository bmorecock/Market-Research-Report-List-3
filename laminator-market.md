<p><p>The laminator market can be segmented into two main types: Pouch Laminators and Roll Laminators. Pouch laminators are designed for smaller scale projects and use pre-sealed pouches to laminate documents. They are often used in homes, small offices, and schools. On the other hand, roll laminators are suited for larger scale and continuous laminating tasks. They use rolls of laminating film and are commonly found in print shops, large offices, and commercial printing facilities. Each type has its own unique features and advantages based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
