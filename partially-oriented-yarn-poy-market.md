<p><p>Partially Oriented Yarn (POY) is a kind of polyester filament yarn primarily used in the manufacturing of textiles and fabrics. POY is obtained through the process of melt spinning, cooling, and winding, which results in semi-crystalline yarn with a slightly higher degree of molecular orientation compared to fully drawn yarn. The properties of POY make it ideal for various applications such as apparel, home textiles, industrial fabrics, and automotive textiles.</p><p>The Partially Oriented Yarn (POY) Market is witnessing steady growth, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5.90% during the forecast period.
