<p><p>Automotive Steering Device market consists of three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Electric Power Steering (EPS). HPS uses hydraulic pressure to assist steering, EHPS combines hydraulic power with electric control, and EPS uses an electric motor for steering assistance. HPS is traditional but effective, EHPS offers improved efficiency, and EPS is the most modern and technologically advanced option. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different driving preferences and needs.</p></p>
