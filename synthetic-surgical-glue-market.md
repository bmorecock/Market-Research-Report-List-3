Cyanoacrylate glue is a fast-drying adhesive commonly used in medical procedures. Polymeric Hydrogel glue provides a flexible and biocompatible bond, ideal for delicate tissues. Urethane-based Adhesive offers strong adhesion and flexibility, making it suitable for a variety of surgical applications. Each type of surgical glue has unique properties that cater to different surgical needs and requirements.</p></p>
