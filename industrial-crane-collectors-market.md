Single Pole Busbar Collector collects power from a single busbar, while Multi-pole Busbar Collector collects power from multiple busbars. Seamless Conductor Rail Collector is a continuous conductor rail system, and Copperhead Conductor Rail Collector uses a copperhead system for power collection.
