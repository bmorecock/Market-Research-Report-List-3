<p><p>Flat Panel Detectors (FPDs) are advanced technology used in medical imaging for detecting X-rays. There are two types of FPD market: Indirect FPD and Direct FPD. Indirect FPDs use a scintillator to convert X-rays into light, which is then converted into electronic signals, while Direct FPDs directly convert X-rays into electronic signals. Indirect FPDs are preferred for their higher image quality and lower radiation dose, while Direct FPDs are more cost-effective and have a simpler design. Both types play a crucial role in improving diagnostic accuracy in medical imaging.</p></p>
