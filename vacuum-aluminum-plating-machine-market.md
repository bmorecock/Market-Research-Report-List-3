<p><p>Suspension Type Vacuum Aluminum Plating Machines are designed to hang and suspend the substrate during the coating process, allowing for better uniformity of the coating. Roller Type Vacuum Aluminum Plating Machines, on the other hand, use a roller system to move the substrate through the coating chamber, providing a continuous and efficient coating process. Both types of machines are popular in the market for their specific advantages, with Suspension Type machines being preferred for delicate or complex substrates and Roller Type machines offering high productivity for mass production.</p></p>
