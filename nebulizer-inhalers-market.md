<p><p>Nebulizer inhalers come in three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh. Pneumatic nebulizer machines use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a breathable mist. Ultrasonic nebulizer machines use vibrations to create aerosol particles for inhalation. Mesh nebulizer machines use a vibrating mesh to generate a fine mist of medication. Each type offers different benefits and caters to specific needs, such as portability, efficiency, and ease of use, making them popular choices in the medical field.</p></p>
