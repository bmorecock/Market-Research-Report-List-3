<p><p>Single drum boilers are categorized into two main types based on their orientation - horizontal boilers and vertical boilers. </p><p>Horizontal boilers have a horizontal arrangement of the drum and are commonly used in industrial applications where space is limited. They are efficient in heat transfer and provide uniform heating throughout the vessel.</p><p>On the other hand, vertical boilers have a vertical arrangement of the drum and are typically used in small space requirements or where height restrictions are present. They are known for their compact design and ease of installation.</p></p>
