Boards below 80 mm are often used for applications requiring lower insulation thickness, such as acoustic insulation in residential buildings. Boards in the 80-150 mm range are commonly used for general thermal and acoustic insulation in commercial buildings. Boards above 150 mm are typically used for industrial applications that require higher levels of insulation. Each type caters to specific needs based on insulation requirements.</p></p>
