<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential Pressure Flow Meters market is categorized into two types, Type I and Type II. Type I meters measure flow rate by calculating the pressure drop across a restriction, such as an orifice plate or venturi tube. Type II meters measure flow rate by detecting the pressure difference between two points in the flow stream. Type I meters are commonly used in industrial applications where high accuracy is required, while Type II meters are more suitable for general flow measurement in various industries.</p></p>
