<p><strong>The Full-lamination Optical Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCA</li><li>OCR</li><li>SCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full-lamination Optical Film Market Types include OCA (Optically Clear Adhesive), OCR (Optically Clear Resin), and SCA (Silicone Compression Adhesive). OCA is a transparent adhesive used to bond the layers of a display panel together, providing clarity and adhesion. OCR is a resin that fills the air gaps between layers, enhancing optical properties and durability. SCA is a silicone adhesive that provides cushioning and shock absorption between layers. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the display industry.</p></p>
