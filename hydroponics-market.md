<p><p>Hydroponics market types include Media Filled Growbeds (MFG), Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), Deep Water Culture (DWC), and others. MFG involves growing plants in a media such as gravel or perlite. NFT circulates a nutrient solution through a shallow channel where plants' roots are suspended. DWC submerges plants' roots in a nutrient solution. Other types may include aeroponics, where plants are grown in a mist environment. Each method offers unique benefits and challenges for cultivating plants hydroponically.</p></p>
