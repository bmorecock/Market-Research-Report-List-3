<p><p>Process gas chromatographs are used in various industries to analyze and monitor gas compounds. There are different types of gas chromatography techniques, including gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). In gas-solid chromatography, the stationary phase is a solid material, while in gas-liquid chromatography, the stationary phase is a liquid coated on a solid support. Both techniques are valuable for separating and identifying gas components based on their different affinities for the stationary phase.</p></p>
