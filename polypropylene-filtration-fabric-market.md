<p><p>Polypropylene Filtration Fabric Market Types include Polypropylene Monofilament Filtration Fabric and Polypropylene Multifilament Filtration Fabric. Monofilament fabric is made from a single continuous strand, providing high tensile strength and excellent cake release properties. Multifilament fabric is made from multiple strands twisted together, offering increased filtration area and better particle retention. Both types are commonly used for industrial filtration applications such as wastewater treatment, food processing, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
