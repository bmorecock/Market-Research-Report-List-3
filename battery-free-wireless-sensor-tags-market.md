<p><p>Battery free wireless sensor tags are classified into three main types: active tags, passive tags, and semi-active tags. Active tags have a built-in power source and can actively transmit data to the reader over long distances. Passive tags rely on the reader's energy to transmit data and have limited range. Semi-active tags are a hybrid of the two, with a power source for sensors but relying on the reader for communication. Each type of tag caters to different market needs based on range, cost, and power requirements.</p></p>
