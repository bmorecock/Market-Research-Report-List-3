<p><p>Stage monitors are essential for performers to hear themselves on stage. There are two main types of stage monitors: Active Monitors and Passive Monitors. Active monitors have built-in amplifiers, making them self-powered and easy to set up. They are ideal for smaller venues or solo performers. Passive monitors, on the other hand, require an external amplifier to function. They are preferred in larger venues or by musicians looking for more customization options. Both types of monitors serve the same purpose of providing clear and accurate sound reinforcement on stage.</p></p>
