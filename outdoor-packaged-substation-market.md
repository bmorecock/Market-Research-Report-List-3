<p><p>Outdoor Packaged Substation market includes liquid-filled transformers and dry type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers use oil or other liquid dielectric materials for insulation and cooling, making them efficient for high voltage applications. Dry type transformers use solid insulation materials, making them safer and more environmentally friendly. Both types are suitable for outdoor installations due to their compact design and weather-resistant enclosures. They cater to different requirements based on the application, location, and safety regulations.</p></p>
