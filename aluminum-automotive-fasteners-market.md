<p><p>Aluminum Automotive Fasteners market offers three main types based on their functionality: Removable Type, Semi-permanent Type, and Permanent Type fasteners. Removable Type fasteners are designed to be easily installed and removed without damaging the components. Semi-permanent Type fasteners provide a balance between easy removal and adequate strength. Permanent Type fasteners are intended to provide a secure and long-lasting connection that is not meant to be removed. Each type caters to different automotive applications depending on the required level of accessibility and strength.</p></p>
