<p><p>Regenerative therapies market consists of three main types: allografts, xenografts, and alloplasts. Allografts are tissue transplants between individuals of the same species, xenografts involve tissue transplants from one species to another, and alloplasts are synthetic materials used in tissue regeneration. Each type of therapy has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different patient needs and preferences. The market for regenerative therapies continues to grow as advancements in technology and research expand treatment options for various medical conditions.</p></p>
