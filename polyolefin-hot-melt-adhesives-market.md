<p><p>Polyolefin hot melt adhesives are available in three main types: granular, blocky, and film. Granular adhesives are small, bead-like particles that are commonly used in bulk adhesive applications. Blocky adhesives are solid blocks or pellets that are melted and applied using specialized equipment. Film adhesives are thin sheets or strips that can be easily cut and applied to surfaces. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
