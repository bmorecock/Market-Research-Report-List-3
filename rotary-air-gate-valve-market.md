<p><p>Rotary air gate valves are widely used in various industries for controlling the flow of materials in pneumatic conveying systems. The two main types are straight rotary air gate valves and side entry rotary air gate valves. Straight rotary air gate valves are installed directly in the pipeline for vertical flow control, while side entry rotary air gate valves are mounted on the side of the pipeline for horizontal flow control. Both types play a crucial role in regulating the movement of materials efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
