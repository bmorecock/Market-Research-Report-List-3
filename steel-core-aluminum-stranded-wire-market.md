<p><p>Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire is a type of overhead conductor used for electrical transmission and distribution. The most common types are ACSR (Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced), ACSR/AW (Aluminum Conductor Aluminum Clad Steel Reinforced), and ACSR/TW (Aluminum Conductor Steel Supported Twisted). ACSR combines the high tensile strength of steel with the lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties of aluminum. ACSR/AW includes an additional aluminum cladding to improve conductivity, while ACSR/TW features a twisted steel core for enhanced flexibility and reduced sag.</p></p>
