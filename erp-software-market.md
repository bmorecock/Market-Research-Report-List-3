<p><p>On-premise ERP software is installed and managed on the company's own servers and infrastructure. This type of ERP software provides full control and customization but requires high upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based ERP software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This type of ERP solution offers lower upfront costs, easier scalability, and automatic updates, but may have limitations in customization and data security. Both market types cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
