<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) products encompass a variety of market types, including resonators, filters, oscillators, transducers, and others. Resonators are used to generate acoustic waves, filters to selectively transmit frequencies, oscillators to generate stable frequencies, and transducers to convert acoustic signals into electrical signals. Other SAW products may include delay lines, temperature sensors, and pressure sensors. These products find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
