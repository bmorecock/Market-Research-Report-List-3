<p><p>Linear regulators, also known as LDO regulators, come in two main market types: PMOS-type LDO and NMOS-type LDO. PMOS-type LDO regulators use PMOS transistors for the pass element, offering low dropout voltage and better performance in low voltage applications. On the other hand, NMOS-type LDO regulators use NMOS transistors for the pass element, providing higher efficiency and better performance in high voltage applications. Both types of LDO regulators play a crucial role in regulating and stabilizing voltage in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
