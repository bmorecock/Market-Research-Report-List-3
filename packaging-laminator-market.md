<p><p>Packaging laminators come in two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic laminators require some manual intervention by the operator, such as loading the materials and adjusting settings, while fully automatic laminators operate without any manual input once set up. Semi-automatic laminators are typically more affordable and versatile, making them suitable for small to medium-sized businesses. Fully automatic laminators, on the other hand, are more efficient and require less supervision, making them ideal for larger-scale operations that require high productivity and precision.</p></p>
