<p><p>Chloroprene Rubber Timing Belts are commonly found in two types: Trapezoidal Tooth and Round Tooth. Trapezoidal Tooth belts have a trapezoidal shape on the teeth, offering higher strength and load-carrying capacity. They are suitable for heavy-duty applications. Round Tooth belts, on the other hand, have a curved tooth profile, providing smoother engagement and reduced noise levels. They are often used in applications where noise and vibration control are important. Both types of timing belts are widely used in various industrial machinery and automotive industries.</p></p>
