<p><strong>The Mica Tape for Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mica Glass Tape</li><li>Mica Polyester Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mica tape for insulation comes in various types, such as Mica Glass Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Tape is made by impregnating glass cloth with synthetic resin and high-quality mica, offering excellent flexibility and heat resistance. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape is made by bonding mica paper with polyester film, providing good dielectric strength and thermal conductivity. Both types of mica tape are widely used in electrical applications for insulation purposes due to their unique properties.</p></p>
