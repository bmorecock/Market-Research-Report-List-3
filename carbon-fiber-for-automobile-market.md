CFRP is widely used for its lightweight and high strength properties, making it ideal for body panels and components. CMCs offer high temperature resistance and are used in engine components. MMCS combine the strength of carbon fiber with the toughness of metals, suitable for structural applications. Each type offers unique advantages for enhancing performance and efficiency in the automotive industry.</p></p>
