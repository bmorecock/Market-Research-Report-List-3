<p><h1>External Pulse Oximeter Market Growth, Market Trends, COVID-19 Impact, and Forecasts for period from 2024 - 2031</h1></p><p><strong>External Pulse Oximeter Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>An external pulse oximeter is a non-invasive medical device used to measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood. It is typically attached to a person's finger, toe, or earlobe and uses light to detect how much oxygen is being carried by the hemoglobin in the red blood cells.</p><p>The external pulse oximeter market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases such as asthma, COPD, and sleep apnea is driving the demand for external pulse oximeters. Additionally, the rising geriatric population and the growing awareness about the importance of monitoring oxygen saturation levels for early detection of health issues are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Advancements in technology, such as the development of wireless and wearable external pulse oximeters, are further driving market growth. These devices offer greater convenience and ease of use for both patients and healthcare providers. Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has also increased the demand for external pulse oximeters, as monitoring oxygen levels has become crucial in the management of the virus.</p><p>Overall, the external pulse oximeter market is expected to continue growing as the importance of monitoring oxygen saturation levels in various healthcare settings becomes increasingly recognized.</p></p>
