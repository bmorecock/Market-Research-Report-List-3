<p><strong>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Standby</li><li>On-line</li><li>Line-Interactive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Market consists of three main types: Passive Standby, On-line, and Line-Interactive. Passive Standby UPS remains inactive until a power outage occurs, then switches to battery power. On-line UPS continuously provides power from the battery, ensuring seamless transition during outages. Line-Interactive UPS offers both battery backup and voltage regulation, adjusting input voltage fluctuations without switching to battery power unless necessary. Each type caters to different military applications, providing varying levels of protection and efficiency.</p></p>
