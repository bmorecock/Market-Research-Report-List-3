<p><p>Automatic Ultrasonic Welders are categorized into two types- Fully Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Fully Automatic Ultrasonic Welders require minimal human intervention as they operate independently throughout the welding process. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic Ultrasonic Welders involve some manual input and supervision from operators. Both types offer efficient and precise welding capabilities, but Fully Automatic Welders are preferred for high-volume production due to their autonomous nature, while Semi-Automatic Welders are suitable for smaller scale operations.</p></p>
