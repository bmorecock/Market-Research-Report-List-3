AAA stent grafts are specifically designed to treat aneurysms in the abdominal region, while TAA stent grafts are used for aneurysms in the thoracic region. Both types of stent grafts are minimally invasive alternatives to traditional open surgery for treating aortic aneurysms. They help to reinforce weakened blood vessels and prevent the risk of rupture, ultimately improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
