<p><p>Medical lateral flow assay (LFA) testing services market offers two main types of assays - Sandwich Assays and Competitive Assays. In Sandwich Assays, the target analyte binds between two layers of antibodies, providing a highly sensitive detection method. On the other hand, Competitive Assays involve competition between the target analyte and a labeled analyte for binding sites on the antibodies, making it suitable for detecting small molecules or haptens. Both types play a crucial role in the growing LFA testing services market.</p></p>
