On the other hand, automatic machines operate without the need for manual intervention, making the process more efficient and less labor-intensive. Both types of machines cater to different market needs, with semi-automatic machines being more suitable for small-scale operations, while automatic machines are preferred for large-scale industrial applications.</p></p>
