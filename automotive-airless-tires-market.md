<p><p>Automotive airless tires are available in two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are made with layers of fabric laid crisscrossed over each other at a 30-40 degree angle, providing a rigid and durable structure. Radial tires have fabric layers positioned at a 90-degree angle from the tread, resulting in better flexibility and increased contact with the road. Both types offer different performance characteristics and are widely used in the automotive industry for various applications.</p></p>
