<p><p>Wellhead equipment and components market includes various types such as hangers, flanges, master valves, and others. Hangers are used to suspend casing strings within the wellbore, while flanges provide a means of connecting and sealing different components of the wellhead assembly. Master valves control the flow of fluids in and out of the wellhead. Other components may include actuators, chokes, and spools among others. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of the wellhead system.</p></p>
