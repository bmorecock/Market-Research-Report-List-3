<p><ul><li>Internally Cooled Cryo Traps</li><li>Externally Cooled Cryo Traps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryo traps are used in various industries for trapping and concentrating volatile compounds. Internally cooled cryo traps have a built-in cooling system that circulates a cryogenic liquid to lower the temperature inside the trap. Externally cooled cryo traps, on the other hand, rely on an external cooling source to maintain a low temperature. Both types of cryo traps are effective in trapping volatile compounds, but the choice between them depends on the specific application and requirements of the user.</p></p>
