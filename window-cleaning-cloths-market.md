<p><p>Window cleaning cloths come in two main market types: multi-component microfiber cloths and mono-component microfiber cloths. Multi-component microfiber cloths are made from a blend of different materials, offering a higher level of absorbency and effectiveness in cleaning windows. On the other hand, mono-component microfiber cloths are made from a single type of material, providing a simpler, more cost-effective option for window cleaning. Both types of cloths are popular choices for consumers looking to achieve streak-free, sparkling clean windows.</p></p>
