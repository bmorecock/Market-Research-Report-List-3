<p><p>Position encoders are sensors used to monitor the position of a rotating or translating object. Angular encoders measure the rotation angle of an object, while rotary encoders track the absolute or relative position of rotating shafts. Linear encoders, on the other hand, measure linear motion along a straight path. These types of encoders are vital in various industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automation, where precise position control is essential for ensuring efficiency and accuracy in operations.</p></p>
