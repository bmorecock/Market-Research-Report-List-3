<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflow oven market includes two main types - convection ovens and vapour phase ovens. Convection ovens use heated air to transfer heat to the components on the circuit board, providing consistent and even heating. Vapour phase ovens, on the other hand, use a vapour medium to transfer heat, ensuring uniform heating and preventing overheating of sensitive components. Both types of ovens are widely used in electronics manufacturing for soldering components onto circuit boards with precision and efficiency.</p></p>
