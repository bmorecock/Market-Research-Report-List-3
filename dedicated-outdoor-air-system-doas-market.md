<p><p>A Dedicated Outdoor Air System (DOAS) is a type of HVAC system that is designed to introduce fresh outdoor air into a building, separate from the air being recirculated within the space. This system helps to maintain indoor air quality by providing a constant supply of fresh air, while also improving energy efficiency by reducing the load on the traditional HVAC system.</p><p>The Dedicated Outdoor Air System (DOAS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period. The increasing focus on energy efficiency and sustainability in buildings, along with growing awareness about indoor air quality, are driving the demand for DOAS systems. Additionally, stringent regulations and building codes mandating the use of such systems in commercial buildings are further propelling market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the DOAS market is the integration of advanced technologies such as sensors, controls, and smart algorithms to optimize system performance and energy savings. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing compact and modular DOAS units that can be easily installed in both new construction and retrofit projects. Overall, the DOAS market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years as more buildings adopt these systems to meet energy efficiency and indoor air quality requirements.</p></p>
