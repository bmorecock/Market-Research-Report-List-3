<p><p>Graphitized cathodes can be categorized into three types based on the degree of graphitization: Semi-graphitic, graphitic, and fully graphitized. Semi-graphitic cathodes have a lower degree of graphitization, making them less conductive. Graphitic cathodes have a moderate level of graphitization, providing better conductivity. Fully graphitized cathodes have the highest degree of graphitization, resulting in superior conductivity and performance. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency and cost, catering to different needs within the market for graphitized cathodes.</p></p>
