<p><p>Precision Link Conveyors come in various types, including Carousel Conveyors and Over-Under Conveyors. Carousel Conveyors are designed to move products in a circular motion, allowing for continuous and efficient transport. On the other hand, Over-Under Conveyors stack products on top of each other, maximizing vertical space usage. Both types of conveyors are commonly used in industries where precision and flexibility in product handling are essential, such as manufacturing and distribution centers.</p></p>
