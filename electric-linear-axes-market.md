<p><p>Electric linear axes come in different types such as toothed belt drive electric linear axis, spindle drive electric linear axis, and driveless linear guide unit. Toothed belt drive axes use a belt to transmit motion, spindle drive axes use a rotating spindle for movement, and driveless linear guide units do not require a separate drive mechanism. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the application, such as speed, precision, and maintenance requirements. Different industries and applications may benefit from using one type over the others.</p></p>
