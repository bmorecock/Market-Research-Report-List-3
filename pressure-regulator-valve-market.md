<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Two-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure regulator valves are categorized into two main types: single-stage and two-stage. Single-stage valves operate by reducing the pressure in one step, directly from the inlet to the desired outlet pressure. Two-stage valves, on the other hand, use a two-step process to achieve the desired outlet pressure, providing more precise control. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing to ensure safe and efficient operation of systems.</p></p>
