<p><p>Chelate Resins Market consists of four main types: Iminodiacetate Type, Polyamine Type, Glucamine Type, and Other types. Iminodiacetate type chelate resins contain multiple acidic groups for strong chelation, while Polyamine type resins have amino groups for metal ion adsorption. Glucamine type resins possess hydroxyl and amine groups for metal complexation. Other types include resins with various functional groups tailored for specific chelating applications. Each type offers unique benefits in metal ion removal and purification processes.</p></p>
