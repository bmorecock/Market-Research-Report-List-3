<p><p>Hot spark plugs are designed to be used in engines that operate at low temperatures. They have a smaller heat range and are more prone to fouling. Cold spark plugs, on the other hand, are used in high-performance engines that operate at high temperatures. They have a larger heat range and are less likely to foul. Both types of spark plugs are important in maintaining the proper combustion process in an engine, but they are optimized for different temperature conditions.</p></p>
