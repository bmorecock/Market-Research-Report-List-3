<p><p>Full bridge driver market types can be categorized into two categories: with PWM and without PWM. Full bridge drivers with PWM functionality allow for precise control of motor speed and direction through pulse width modulation signals. On the other hand, full bridge drivers without PWM are more basic in functionality and do not offer the same level of speed control. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application and the desired level of motor control.</p></p>
