<p><p>Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) current sensors are available in two main types: Digital Output and Analog Output. Digital Output current sensors provide a binary signal indicating the presence or absence of current above a certain threshold, while Analog Output sensors provide a continuous output signal proportional to the magnitude of the current being measured. Both types offer high sensitivity and accuracy in current sensing applications, with Digital Output sensors providing a simple on/off indication and Analog Output sensors offering more precise measurement and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
