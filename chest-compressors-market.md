<p><p>Chest compressors are devices used for providing chest compressions during cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR). There are two main types of chest compressors available in the market: mechanical and electric. Mechanical chest compressors are operated manually by the user, applying pressure to the chest to simulate the actions of CPR. Electric chest compressors, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and provide automated chest compressions at a consistent rate and depth. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper chest compressions during emergencies.</p></p>
