<p><p>Mobile Peer-To-Peer (P2P) Money Transfer Market includes two main types: Remote Payment and Proximity Payment. Remote Payment involves transferring money between two parties who are not physically present in the same location, typically done through mobile apps. Proximity Payment, on the other hand, allows for the transfer of money between individuals who are in close proximity to each other, usually using Near Field Communication (NFC) technology. Both types have their own benefits and use cases in the mobile money transfer market.</p></p>
