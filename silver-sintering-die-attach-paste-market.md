<p><p>Silver sintering die attach paste is used in two main types of markets: pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. In the pressure sintering market, the paste is compressed between the die and substrate under high pressure and temperature to create a strong bond. In the pressure-less sintering market, the paste is heated without applying pressure, allowing the particles to diffuse and create a bond. Each type of market offers unique benefits and is chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
