<p><p>Remote interpretation refers to language interpretation services provided through a remote platform. Simultaneous interpreting involves the interpreter speaking at the same time as the speaker, commonly used in conferences or large events. Consecutive interpretation involves the interpreter speaking after the speaker has finished, commonly used in meetings or discussions. Both types of interpretation have their own specific market demand, with simultaneous interpreting being more prevalent in larger events and consecutive interpretation being more common in smaller, more interactive settings.</p></p>
