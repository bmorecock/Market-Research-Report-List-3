<p><strong>The Audio Amplifier ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio amplifier ICs are commonly available in two market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount ICs are smaller in size and can be mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, making them ideal for compact devices with limited space. On the other hand, Through Hole ICs have leads that are inserted through holes in the circuit board and soldered into place, providing a stronger mechanical connection. Both types offer different benefits and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
