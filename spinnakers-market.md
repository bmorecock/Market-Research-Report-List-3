<p><strong>The Spinnakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Symmetric Spinnakers</li><li>Asymmetric Spinnakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Symmetric spinnakers are traditional downwind sails that are flown directly opposite the mainsail on a fixed pole. They are typically used in symmetrical downwind sailing conditions. Asymmetric spinnakers are more modern downwind sails that are flown from a retractable bow sprit or other fixed points on the boat. They are designed for reaching and running angles and are easier to handle than symmetric spinnakers. Both spinnakers provide additional downwind sail area and help maximize boat speed in light wind conditions.</p></p>
