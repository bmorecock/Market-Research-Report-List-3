<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital gas meters are classified into two main market types: Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR system allows gas utilities to remotely collect consumption data and perform basic functions without physical meter reading. On the other hand, AMI system enables two-way communication between the gas meter and the utility, providing real-time data such as gas usage patterns and potential leaks. Both systems offer enhanced efficiency and accuracy in gas metering operations, benefiting both consumers and utility providers.</p></p>
