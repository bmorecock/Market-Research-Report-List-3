<p><p>Point Level Monitoring in the wireless tank level monitoring system market is a type of monitoring where sensors are placed at specific points in the tank to indicate when the level reaches a certain threshold. On the other hand, Continuous Level Monitoring involves sensors that provide real-time data on the exact level of the tank at all times. Both types serve different purposes, with Point Level Monitoring being more cost-effective for simple applications, while Continuous Level Monitoring is ideal for industries requiring precise and constant monitoring.</p></p>
