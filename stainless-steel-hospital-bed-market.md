Electric beds have motorized controls for easy adjustment of the bed's position. Semi-electric beds combine manual and electric features, with electric controls for adjusting the head and foot sections and manual controls for adjusting the height. Each type offers different levels of convenience and functionality to meet the varying needs of patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
