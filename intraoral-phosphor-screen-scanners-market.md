<p><p>Intraoral phosphor screen scanners come in two main types: pointer type and digital type. Pointer type scanners use a handheld device to capture images by pointing at the desired area, allowing for precise and targeted image acquisition. Digital type scanners, on the other hand, have a fixed image capture area and rely on digital technology to capture and display the images. Both types of scanners are used in the dental industry for capturing detailed images of the inside of the mouth for diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
