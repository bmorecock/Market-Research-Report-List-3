<p><p>The continuous environmental disinfection system market offers two main types: single mode and dual mode. Single mode systems use a single technology or method for disinfection, such as UV light or air filtration. Dual mode systems combine two different technologies or methods for more comprehensive disinfection, like UV light and chemical disinfectants. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with single mode systems being simpler and more cost-effective, while dual mode systems offer enhanced effectiveness and flexibility in disinfection.</p></p>
