<p><strong>The Particle Size Analyzing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Diffraction</li><li>Dynamic Light Scattering</li><li>Imaging Analysis</li><li>Coulter Principle</li><li>Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particle size analyzing equipment market consists of various types such as Laser Diffraction, Dynamic Light Scattering, Imaging Analysis, Coulter Principle, Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis, and Others. Laser Diffraction measures particle size by analyzing the diffraction pattern produced when light passes through a sample. Dynamic Light Scattering measures particle size by analyzing the fluctuations in light scattering caused by Brownian motion. Imaging analysis uses cameras to capture images of particles for size analysis. Coulter Principle measures particle size by detecting changes in electrical impedance as particles pass through a small opening. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis tracks the movement of individual particles to determine size. Other methods include techniques such as Sedimentation, Sieve Analysis, and Laser Obscuration. Each type of equipment offers unique benefits for specific particle size analysis needs.</p></p>
