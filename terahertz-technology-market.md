<p><p>Terahertz technology market can be divided into two main types: terahertz sources and terahertz detectors. Terahertz sources refer to devices that generate terahertz radiation, such as quantum cascade lasers or photoconductive antennas. On the other hand, terahertz detectors are used to measure and detect terahertz signals, like bolometers or Schottky diodes. Both markets are critical for the advancement of terahertz technology and find applications in various fields like security screening, medical imaging, and material characterization.</p></p>
