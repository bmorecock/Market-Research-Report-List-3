<p><ul><li>Manual Starter</li><li>Magnetic Motor Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual starters require the user to manually initiate the motor start-up process by physically turning a knob or switch. These devices are simple to operate and are commonly used in small, low-power applications. On the other hand, magnetic motor starters use an electromagnet to control the flow of electricity to the motor. These starters are more complex than manual starters and are typically used in larger, higher-power applications where automated control is preferred. Both types of starters play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient motor operation.</p></p>
