<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are used to measure the time taken for a signal to travel from the sensor to an object and back, helping in determining distance. </p><p>Direct ToF sensors directly measure the time of flight of the signal, providing accurate distance measurements. Indirect ToF sensors use other methods such as phase-shift or interferometry to estimate the time of flight.</p><p>Both types of ToF sensors are widely used in applications such as robotics, automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
