<p><p>Portable ambulatory ventilators are devices that assist individuals with breathing difficulties by providing supplemental oxygen or by mechanically assisting with breathing. These ventilators come in three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators use electricity to power the device, providing precise control over breaths. Pneumatic ventilators use air pressure to deliver breaths, while electro-pneumatic ventilators combine electronic and pneumatic components for enhanced performance. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different patient needs.</p></p>
