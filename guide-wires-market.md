Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on producing guide wires with improved flexibility, pushability, and torqueability to enhance procedural outcomes and patient safety.</p><p>Another trend shaping the guide wires market is the increasing adoption of disposable guide wires to reduce the risk of cross-contamination and infection. This shift towards single-use devices is likely to drive market growth as healthcare facilities prioritize patient safety and infection control measures.</p></p>
