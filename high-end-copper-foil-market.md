<p><strong>The High-end Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-end copper foil market includes two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced by rolling copper ingots through a series of rolling mills to reduce the thickness and improve the surface finish. It is commonly used in flexible printed circuits and electromagnetic shielding. Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced by electrodeposition, resulting in a high-quality, pure copper foil with excellent conductivity. It is often used in high-tech applications such as lithium-ion batteries and semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
