<p><p>Glass vaccine vials are primarily available in two types - Molded Glass Vials and Tubular Glass Vials. Molded Glass Vials are made through a molding process where glass is shaped into a vial form. These vials are known for their durability and precision in design. On the other hand, Tubular Glass Vials are manufactured using a tubular glass tube which is then cut and formed into vials. These vials are commonly used for high-volume vaccines due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of production.</p></p>
