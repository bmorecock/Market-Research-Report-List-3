<p><p>Apnea monitors are devices used to monitor breathing patterns during sleep to detect and diagnose sleeping disorders such as sleep apnea. The market for apnea monitors includes three main types: Single Channel Screening Devices, which provide basic monitoring of breathing patterns; Actigraphy Systems, which track movement and activity during sleep to assess sleep quality; and PSG Devices, which are comprehensive diagnostic tools used in sleep laboratories to measure various parameters such as brain activity, eye movements, and muscle tone during sleep.</p></p>
