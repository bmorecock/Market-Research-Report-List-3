<p><p>Transfer switches market consists of two main types - Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) and Manual Transfer Switches. Automatic Transfer Switches are generally used in commercial and industrial settings where power outages can have a significant impact on operations. They automatically switch between power sources to ensure uninterrupted power supply. On the other hand, Manual Transfer Switches require the user to physically switch between power sources, making them more suitable for residential or small-scale applications where power reliability is not as critical.</p></p>
