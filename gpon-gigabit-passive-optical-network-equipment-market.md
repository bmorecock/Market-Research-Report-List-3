<p><p>GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) Equipment market includes two main types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT) and Optical Network Terminal (ONT). OLTs are located at the service provider's central office and are responsible for managing connections to multiple ONTs. ONTs, on the other hand, are located at the customer's premises and provide the interface between the customer's devices and the GPON network. Both OLTs and ONTs play a crucial role in enabling high-speed internet access and other telecommunications services over fiber optic networks.</p></p>
