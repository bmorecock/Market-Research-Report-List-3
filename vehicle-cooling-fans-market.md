<p><p>Vehicle cooling fans are essential components in engine cooling systems. There are two main types of cooling fans in the market: mechanical radiator fans and electric radiator fans. Mechanical radiator fans are driven by the engine via a belt and clutch system, providing effective cooling at high speeds. On the other hand, electric radiator fans are powered by an electric motor and can be controlled independently, offering better efficiency and flexibility in cooling performance. Both types play a crucial role in preventing engine overheating and ensuring optimal vehicle performance.</p></p>
