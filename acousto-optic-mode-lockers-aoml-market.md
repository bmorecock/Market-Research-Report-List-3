<p><p>Acousto-Optic Mode Lockers (AOML) are devices used in lasers to achieve mode locking, which produces short pulses with high peak power. There are two main types of AOML markets: Free-Space Acousto-Optic Mode Lockers and Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Mode Lockers. Free-Space AOMLs are used in open-air laser setups, while Fiber-Coupled AOMLs are integrated into optical fiber systems. Both types play essential roles in various applications such as laser micromachining, spectroscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
