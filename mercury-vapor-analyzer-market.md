<p><p>Mercury Vapor Analyzer is a device used to detect and measure mercury concentrations in various environments. The two main types of Mercury Vapor Analyzers are Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption and Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence. Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption utilizes a light source to measure the absorption of mercury vapor, while Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence measures the fluorescence emitted by mercury atoms. Both methods are highly sensitive and accurate in detecting low levels of mercury contamination in air, water, and soil samples.</p></p>
